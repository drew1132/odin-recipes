Tacos

Description

Easy, Tasty Tacos Made at Home!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Ground Beef
  • 2 Teaspoons Chili Powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Powder, Oregano, and Cumin
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Onion Powder, Salt, and Pepper
  • 1/2 Cup Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 8 Corn Or Flour Tortillas
  • Toppings As Desired: Cheese, Lettuce, Tomatoes, Etc.

Steps

  1. Cook and Drain Ground Beef
  2. Add Seasoning, Tomato Sauce, and Water To Ground Beef
  3. Cook For 5 Minutes, Stirring Often
  4. Add Taco Meat To Warmed Tortillas
  5. Add Toppings As Desired and Serve
