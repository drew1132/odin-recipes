Alfredo Sauce

Description

Easy, Creamy Alfredo Sauce Made at Home!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1 1/2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 Cups Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 Teaspoons Garlic
  • Salt & Pepper To Taste

Steps

  1. Add butter and cream to large skillet.
  2. Simmer over low heat for 2 minutes.
  3. Whisk in garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Whisk in parmesan until melted.
  5. Serve immediately.
